Welcome to the suburb of the future.

Surrounded by parklands of green open spaces, YarraBend is a visionary, riverside suburb just 6.5km from Melbourne CBD. With its unique combination of open spaces and cutting edge design, YarraBend is where new technology and old traditions meet.

YarraBend will be a world first TESLA suburb by including Tesla Powerwalls within houses. YarraBend has been designed to achieve the highest level of sustainability and quality of life.
