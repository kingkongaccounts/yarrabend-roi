Lush green spaces.

YarraBend is surrounded by Darebin Parklands and Yarra Bend Park, with views over endless canopies of native trees. Here you can lose yourself walking in the natural bushland or jogging past the area’s last remaining orchards.

Future focused.

With a strong focus around sustainability and design, at YarraBend innovation leads the way ensuring its future-proofed. From innovations like PV energy and TESLA Powerwalls to meticulous horticultural designs and the latest in street design, YarraBend will be a green and sustainable place that exists in harmony with the natural environment.

Shopping destination.

With everything from major supermarkets to chic designer boutiques, YarraBend will cater to every need of its new and diverse community. Local fashion brands high-end labels, gift shops and florists will all create a village atmosphere in the heart of YarraBend.

Local knowledge.

As well as offering its own modern primary school, YarraBend puts you within easy reach of the highest concentration of well-regarded educational institutions in Australia, from kindergartens through to private school VCE.

On the edge of Kew.

YarraBend is conveniently located only 450m from Alphington Railway Station, with platforms shaded by heritage listed trees Red Gums that pre-date European settlement. Fairfield Railway Station, adjacent to charming shopping village is also less than 15 minutes away by foot.

Arts & culture.

Art will be a major focus in the design of YarraBend, with an Art and Design Precinct that celebrates local and international artists through exhibitions, workshops and interactive pop-ups.

YarraBend is a lushly landscaped neighbourhood where residents can wander along paths through leafy parks, community gardens or along the spectacular elevated park, The Paper Trail.

With rooftop terraces, bike paths, playgrounds, riverside trails and three expansive gardens, this is a place to enjoy fresh air and time outdoors at your doorstep.

Along the streets and laneways of YarraBend, history and heritage will interweave with the latest technology. Here, a holistic lifestyle will be enhanced by the latest ideas in health and sustainability and a rich culture of art and craftsmanship.

Smartwired.

On the cutting edge of connectivity, YarraBend residents will belong to a Smartwired community with high-speed internet and a complimentary tech-concierge, a technical call-out service to assist with smart wiring or even WiFi home entertainment set ups.

A unique YarraBend app will also connect residents to a variety of amenities and information within the community, including public transport timetables, home delivery menus, carpooling arrangements and social events.

Health & Wellness.

YarraBend has been conceived as a peaceful place to live, away from the frenetic pace of city life. Nature can be experienced in all its delight, whether on a riverside stroll or in the communal garden.

Bike paths meander throughout the precinct and connect right through to the city. Running tracks and the Paper Trail, an elevated park, will connect to a range of outdoor gym equipment. Various sporting facilities encourage social games fitness, including multipurpose courts, ovals, golf, swimming, a wellness centre and a fully equipped gymnasium. Children will also enjoy the many playgrounds and parks, with plenty of room to run and play.

Exceptional connectivity.

Within YarraBend, walkways and bike paths enjoy priority over vehicular roads. Inspired by Scandinavian bike-friendly cities, YarraBend’s kerbless street design promotes greater safety and respect between pedestrians, cyclists and drivers.

YarraBend is only 450m from Alphington Station, shaded by heritage-listed Red Gums, with Fairfield Station less than 15 minutes away on foot. Buses commute directly to Queen Victoria Market and Melbourne University in the city, while those who drive have easy access to the Eastern Freeway across a new Chandler Highway bridge.

YarraBend will be home to a range of thoughtfully designed spaces and residences, from luxurious riverfront townhouses to contemporary apartments and studios.

Premium homes.

YarraBend offers a range of premium, architecturally designed residences to suit different needs. Choose from townhomes by the river, designer apartments or build your dream home with multi-zoned living areas, alfresco entertainment, stone benchtops and European appliances.

YarraBend residences are architecturally designed. Endurance and the highest level of innovation and sustainability has been considered features such energy efficient lighting, Solar PV energy and Tesla Powerwalls.
