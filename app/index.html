Welcome to the suburb of the future.

Surrounded by parkland, YarraBend is a visionary, riverside suburb only 10 minutes from Melbourne. With its unique combination of open spaces and cutting edge design, YarraBend will reinvent the way people live, work and have fun.

YarraBend will be the first site to achieve a 6 out of 6 UDIA rating with a future-proofed neighbourhood that features everything from electric car charge points to energy efficient lighting. Solar PV energy will be used throughout YarraBend, with innovative Tesla Powerwalls allowing residents to optimise their solar energy use.

YarraBend will also bring gourmet grocers, and artisan food makers dedicated to their craft to share old traditions and new flavours with the local community. Along with everything from major supermarkets to chic designer boutiques, YarraBend will cater to every need of its new and diverse community.

The Masterplan.

A Smartwired suburb.

As a suburb of the future, YarraBend promises to put its residents at the cutting edge of connectivity. This Smartwired community will enjoy high-speed internet, allowing instant access and fast downloads.

YarraBend residents will also be entitled to a complimentary tech-concierge. This technical call-out service can be used whenever residents choose within the first year of occupancy. This could include anything from assistance with smart wiring and Wi-Fi to setting up home entertainment systems.

Residents will also be able to download the unique YarraBend app, which will provide everything from public transport timetables, home delivery menus and carpooling information to social connections with the YarraBend community.

Healthy living inside and out.

YarraBend has been conceived as a peaceful place to live, away from the frenetic pace of city life. Nature can be experienced in all its delightful forms, whether on a riverside stroll or by harvesting the fruits of the communal garden.

Bike baths throughout the precinct will be dotted with rest stops, while running tracks and the highline trail will connect with a range of outdoor gym equipment.

Exceptional connectivity.

YarraBend is conveniently located only 450m from Alphington Railway Station, with platforms shaded by heritage listed trees Red Gums that pre-date European settlement. Fair eld Railway Station, adjacent to charming shopping village is also less than 15 minutes away by foot.

Even closer to home, a bus stop immediately outside YarraBend will take residents directly to Queen Victoria Market and Melbourne University. YarraBend is of course easily accessed by car, with the Eastern Freeway just across a new Chandler Highway bridge.

Walkways and bike paths are designed to take priority over roads for cars, with YarraBend also embracing the latest trends in street design. The use of kerbless roads, an idea that has ourished in Scandinavian countries, will promote safe and social relationships between pedestrians, drivers and cyclists.
